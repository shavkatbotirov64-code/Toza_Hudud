BROWSER CONSOLE'DA BAJARING:
================================

1. F12 bosing (DevTools ochish)
2. Console tab'ga o'ting
3. Quyidagi kodni yozing va Enter bosing:

localStorage.clear(); 
sessionStorage.clear();
location.reload();

================================

Bu barcha eski ma'lumotlarni o'chiradi va sahifani qayta yuklaydi.

TEKSHIRISH:
================================

Sahifa qayta yuklangandan keyin:

1. Quti yashil bo'lishi kerak (fillLevel: 15%)
2. Mashinalar patrol qilishi kerak (quti atrofida aylanishi)
3. Console'da "binStatus: EMPTY" ko'rinishi kerak

Agar hali ham muammo bo'lsa:
- Ctrl+Shift+R (hard refresh)
- Yoki browser cache'ni to'liq tozalang
