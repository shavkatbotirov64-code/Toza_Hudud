FRONTEND NI HOZIR DEPLOY QILISH

=== USUL 1: Railway Dashboard (TAVSIYA ETILADI) ===

1. Brauzerda oching: https://railway.com/project/e0ee489f-1abf-4ca2-8a38-9eb343401483

2. Login: saidkarimcyber01@gmail.com

3. "Toza_Hudud_frontend" service ni bosing

4. Yuqori o'ng burchakda "Deploy" tugmasini bosing
   YO
   "Deployments" tabga o'ting va oxirgi deployment ni "Redeploy" qiling

5. Build jarayonini kuzating (2-3 daqiqa)

6. Deploy tugagandan keyin brauzerda:
   - https://tozahududfrontend-production.up.railway.app
   - Ctrl+Shift+R (hard refresh)
   - F12 > Console > Sensor loglarini ko'ring


=== USUL 2: Terminal (AGAR USUL 1 ISHLAMASA) ===

cd frontend
railway link
# "Toza_Hudud" project ni tanlang
# "production" environment ni tanlang  
# "Toza_Hudud_frontend" service ni tanlang
railway up --detach

=== AGAR HALI HAM ISHLAMASA ===

Railway da "Watch Paths" sozlamalarini tekshiring:
- Settings > Watch Paths
- Bo'sh bo'lishi kerak (barcha fayllarni kuzatish uchun)

Yoki:
- Settings > Deploy Trigger
- "Automatic" bo'lishi kerak
